HANSOM is the leading company that continues the culture of Korea.

HANSOM is a leading company that sells Korean traditional food, such as meal, dessert, tea, beverage and liqour cleanly and safely.

In addition, we have been recognized as a leader in the industry by various certifications and designations including designated excellent cultural products by the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism, a certification of traditional food quality by the Ministry for Food, Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries, Selected as a New Intellectual of Korea, the 21st Korean food master.

HANSOM is always honest with its sense of mission to continue Korean culture, not just food.

HANSOM is walking on a path of silence with the pride of the Korean Food Grand Master who does not compromise with convenience, and the sense of mission to continue tradition rather than profit making.

HANSOM has been striving for the succession, development and popularization of our traditional food culture.

Through continuous innovation and research and development, we are making every effort to earn the value of excellent and beautiful Korean food culture.

HANSOM sells products that are full of nutrients that are good for our health.

HANSOM sells products made with carefully selected ingredients, various grains, and nuts grown on our land.

The Korean Food Grand Master selects and shapes the ingredients with the wisdom of understanding the value of nature that nature has given to us, in the spirit of creating a culture that embraces our nation’s emotions and souls, rather than just simple food.

HANSOM will provide a way for the future of traditional foods and will lead a healthy food culture of modern people.
